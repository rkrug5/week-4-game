jQuery with Gems!

Instructions:

You will be given a random Target Number at the start of the game.

There are four gems below. By clicking on a gem, you will add a specific amount of points to your Current Total.

You win the game by matching your Current Total to the Target Number. You will lose if you go over the given amount.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
